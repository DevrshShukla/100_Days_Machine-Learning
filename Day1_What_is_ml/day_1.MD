## ü§ñ What is Machine Learning?

**Machine Learning (ML)** is a subset of **Artificial Intelligence (AI)** that enables systems to learn patterns from data and improve automatically through experience, without being explicitly programmed for every scenario.

> ‚ÄúMachine learning gives computers the ability to learn without being explicitly programmed.‚Äù

Instead of writing rules manually, we let the machine **discover rules from data**.


## üìä Types of Machine Learning (Overview)

> These are introduced conceptually and will be covered in detail later in the series.

### 1. Supervised Learning
- Data is labeled
- Example: Email spam detection

### 2. Unsupervised Learning
- Data is unlabeled
- Example: Customer segmentation

### 3. Reinforcement Learning
- Learning through rewards and penalties
- Example: Game-playing AI
